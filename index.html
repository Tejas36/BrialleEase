Our Products

We have made a number of devices and online websites to help people learn braille

Braille Embosser

BrailleEase is a revolutionary handheld braille embossing device designed for on-the-go use. With its compact and lightweight design, it allows users to create high-quality braille documents anywhere, anytime. Easy to operate, BrailleEase features intuitive controls and a comfortable grip, making it perfect for visually impaired individuals seeking independence. Whether at home, in the classroom, or on the move, BrailleEase empowers users to quickly produce braille text, enhancing literacy and accessibility.

Website Translator

It is a side product of our BrailleEase Braille Embossing Device which provides a platform for you to either practice your catch on braille or translate text to braille or braille to text. For a blind person this website has an inbuilt option of listening to our speech and translating it to english then you can further translate it to braille. It has a read aloud option so that they can hear the results.

20 Lessons On English Braille

A 90 paged document consisting of how to write Braille in 20 lessons. Consists of Definitions, Exercises, Slate Work, Key to Exercises.
